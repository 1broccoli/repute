Version 3.0 - February 5, 2026
- Custom experience messages with bonus indicators (gb for group bonus, rb for rested bonus)
- Toggle between compact (1.2k) or full (1200) number formats for experience and reputation
- New "Hide Minimap Icon" option to declutter your interface
- Auto-track reputation gains in your watch bar for easier monitoring
- Improved stability and performance fixes for TBC Anniversary compatibility

- Added "Hide Minimap Icon" option in settings window
- Added "Auto-Track Reputation Gains" option in settings window
- When enabled, automatically tracks reputation in the watch bar when you gain rep (excluding honor)
- Both settings persist across game sessions
